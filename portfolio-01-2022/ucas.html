UX/UI Designer

UCAS

We were tasked with making user focussed improvements to the course search sections of the UCAS web estate.

Project length: 7 months
Tags: Web, Higher Education, UX Design, Web design

We had a wealth of user data to draw from in order to begin. We used Google Analytics data, feedback gathered through the website and through school visits, and we were able to use tools such as Hotjar to get a further insight into user behaviour and pain points.

After defining the key issues to solve based on impact and ease of implementation, we began wireframing, prototyping and creating high fidelity mockups for various sections of the website which were owned by different development teams (this required a lot of organisation and patience!).

As we created concepts and plans for these new pages, we validated with users through a mix of online and in-person guerilla feedback.

In the end we made a variety of improvements ranging from making text more readable in general to combining previously segmented areas of the website.
